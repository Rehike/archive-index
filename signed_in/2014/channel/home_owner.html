    • Add web links

      Let viewers know where else to find you on the web by adding links in the "About Tab".

      Give your channel a custom look by adding channel art, which shows on all devices. Go to the top of your channel to add it.

    • Describe your channel

      Let viewers know what your channel's all about. Click "+ channel description" on your channel's About tab.

    • Add a section

      Use sections to organise your videos and playlists for viewers. Try clicking "add a section" at the bottom of your channel home.

      Introduce your channel to non-subscribed viewers and encourage them to subscribe by adding a short channel trailer.
